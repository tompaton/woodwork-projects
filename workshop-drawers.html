Workshop Drawers

Drawers for screws/nails etc.

Dividers in the drawers are a bit easier and hopefully more flexible than many small apothecary-style drawers.  Can also add a second "till" layer in each draw for more storage.

Made from old plywood from a tabletop.  Too old, as the plywood was delaminating in places and needed a lot of repair and more care than I gave it.

Made the drawers first to get the dimensions for the carcase.

Should have put more thought into it, as I was trying to find a (blind) joint to work in the plywood, but the sliding dovetail doesn't play well with a drawer bottom in grooves...
