Art supply tote box

A box for storing/carrying art supplies (paint tubes) modeled on a Japanese style toolbox.

Similar box by John Zhu here https://youtu.be/pxa88seXsNc?si=2QYJmRWRqUD6E20g&t=567

I made this from reclaimed timber from a chest of drawers.  The wood is quite soft, but nice and light which is important so the box isn't too heavy.

Preliminary design

Larger box on the bottom, with a handle.  A slightly smaller box sits on top of this as a lid.  There is also a small till in the bottom box for paint brushes etc.

Cutting and assembling the boxes

Glued up and ready to finish

Finished with boiled linseed oil and shellac
