2022 Tool restoration

A few small tool cleanups/builds from late 2022.

Stanley level - cleaned up and tuned

Winding sticks - new build from some Tasmanian oak, inset magnets to snap them together for storage.

Anchor rabbet/fillister plane depth stop - Similar to a Stanley #78, this plane didn't have a depth stop so I managed to crudely cut some bits of brass and solder them together into something workable.
